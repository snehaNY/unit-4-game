CANDY COLLECTOR

You will be given a random number at start of the game.

There are four candies below. By clicking on a candy you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above random number.

The valus of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each candy
